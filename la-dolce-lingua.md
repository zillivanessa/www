<!-- section: method -->
<!-- lang: en -->
La Dolce Lingua draws on three traditions: sensory learning, narrative immersion, and the Montessori principle that children thrive when they're free to explore, touch, and discover at their own pace.

**Method Pills:**
- Sensory approach
- Storytelling & narrative
- Montessori-inspired
- Voice & movement
- Real objects, no toys
- Human connection first

<!-- lang: it -->
La Dolce Lingua si ispira a tre tradizioni: apprendimento sensoriale, immersione narrativa e il principio montessoriano per cui i bambini fioriscono quando esplorano, toccano e scoprono al proprio ritmo.

**Pillole Metodo:**
- Approccio sensoriale
- Narrazione e storie
- Ispirato a Montessori
- Voce e movimento
- Oggetti reali
- Prima la relazione

<div class="game-card">
  <div class="game-num">02</div>
  <h3>Memory tattile</h3>
  <p>Pairs of cards with different textures — rough, smooth, soft, bumpy — each associated with an Italian adjective. Children feel and say the word, linking language to sensation.</p>
</div>
<div class="game-card">
  <div class="game-num">03</div>
  <h3>Caccia al tesoro lessicale</h3>
  <p>A word treasure hunt hidden around the room. Children search for objects whose Italian names they've been learning, then bring them back and name them aloud — movement, memory, and language all at once.</p>
